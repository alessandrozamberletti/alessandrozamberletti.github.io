Alessandro Zamberletti
Ph.D. Student @ University of Insubria

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Applied Recognition Technology research laboratory under the supervision of Prof. Ignazio Gallo. My research interests are in text localization and recognition from natural images and object class segmentation.
